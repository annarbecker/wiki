Divs and Spans

Definition

Divs are used to give a class to an entire block. Spans are used to give a class to inline elements.

Code Example

<div class="serif">

<h1> Hey Friends! </h1>

<p> Lorem ipsum <span class="red">dolor</span> sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua. </p>

</div>
